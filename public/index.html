Kidderminster Aid in Sickness Fund (KAiSF)

KAiSF is a charity that provides monetary grants to those both suffering from ill health and in financial need. Applicants must live in the former borough of Kidderminster in Worcestershire, UK.

How to apply....

Please email support@kaisf.org.uk for the grant application form.
